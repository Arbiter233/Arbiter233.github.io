C1.3 MAC (continued)

MAC message authentication codes

Description: use symmetric key for parties to verify integrity and authenticity of msg.

  • common: HMAC(with SHA2), KMAC(with SHA3)
  • HMAC: hash concatenation H(k1 || msg) = h1
    H(k2 || h1) = h iteratively
  • classical usage: web cookies, passsword storage
  • avoid directly usage of SHA256 to build MAC, use HMAC
